About SarkariJobListing

SarkariJobListing helps you search for latest government jobs and provides free job alerts, covering:

1. Ministries/ Departments/ Offices/ Organizations/ Autonomous bodies/ Societies/ PSUs of the Central Government, State Government and UT Administrations.
2. Nationalised Banks/ RRBs /UPSC/SSC/ Constitutional and Statutory bodies.
3. Central/State Governments Universities/ Colleges/Institutes recognised by the UGC/AICTE.

We also cover jobs published in Rojgar Samachar aka Employment News published by Government of India and 9,000+ websites of the Government of India.

Search Job - Using Keywords

You can search for jobs by entering simple keywords(s) relating to education, government department, state name , location, pre-qualification criteria etc. Sarkari Jobs app will filter jobs, having keywords anywhere in the job notification. Examples of searches - Railway recruitment 2019, Banking Jobs 2019, UPSC, GATE, PSU Jobs 2019, Teaching Jobs etc.
